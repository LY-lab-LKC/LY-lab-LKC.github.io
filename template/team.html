Our Group

Team Leader / Assistant Professor

Lynn Yap

Assistant Professor

I'm an Assistant Professor at the Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine with a joint appointment at the National Heart Research Institute Singapore. I lead a team focused on exploring the molecular and physiological mechanisms behind stem cell therapies in regenerative cardiology. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies, we seek to advance cardiometabolic medicine and drive future innovations in healthcare.

Research Fellows

Swarnaseetha Adusumalli

Senior Research Fellow

I am a Bioinformatician with extensive experience in high-throughput omics data analysis and integration. My research focuses on the differentiation processes of cardiomyocytes, photoreceptors, and retinal ganglion cells, utilizing bulk, single-cell, and spatial transcriptomics in laminin-cultured cells. I use advanced bioinformatics approaches to understand and elucidate the underlying mechanisms of retinal ganglions and cardiac maturation and regeneration.

Alumni

Cheryl Lee

Alumni

I am an SBS Y3 undergraduate working on my FYP project that involves studying the effects of thyroid hormone receptor and thyroid hormone on the regulation of metabolic maturation of hPSC-derived cardiomyocytes. Through the over-expression of the receptor in cardiomyocytes, I perform metabolic assays, such as the Seahorse assay, with the aim to quantify the extent in which this over-expression contributes to metabolic maturation of cardiomyocytes.
